Step 1

Press 'Space' on your keyboard to play repeated noise.

Press 'Escape' to stop the noise from being played.

Step 2

Next, try to locate the repeat that you heard in the noise by filtering its frequency.

You can do this by moving the grey rectangle on the canvas below.

You can filter higher frequencies by moving the rectangle upwards

Move the position of the rectangle with the following arrows ↑↓

Control the height of the rectangle using 'h'.

Press the 'Shift' key once to alter between increasing or decreasing the height.
